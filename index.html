About Me

Hello World! My name is Sevilla Smith, and I’m currently studying Computer Science at the University of Arizona. Before my technical journey, I had a deep-rooted passion for art, design, and writing, which has always driven my value of creativity. After taking a summer course on Python, I discovered another type of creativity through code and problem-solving, and I’ve been hooked ever since! With a background in art and a rising skill set in coding, I’m driven to participate in projects that allow me to blend these two creative mediums together. Whether it's developing visually appealing websites, innovative software, user-friendly interfaces, or even video games, I’m excited to discover opportunities that celebrate being artistically inspired and technically advanced!
